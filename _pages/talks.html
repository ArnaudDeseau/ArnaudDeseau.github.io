Land Reform and Productivity: Evidence from the Dissolution of French Monasteries
I constructed a new database from both primary and secondary sources on the income of French monasteries in year 1768. I combine these data to calculate the monastic income exposure of each arrondissement before the French Revolution, proxying the local economic importance of monasteries and the effect of the reallocation of their properties after the Revolution.

Speed of Convergence in a Malthusian World: Weak or Strong Homeostasis?
With Adam Levai and Michèle Schmiegelow, I constructed a new database on the total number of professional judges per 100,000 inhabitants by collecting data from various public institutions. We draw on those data as a proxy for the historical evolution of ATJ from 1970 through 2014 for a wide range of developed and developing countries.
