Access to Justice
With Adam Levai and Michèle Schmiegelow, I constructed a new database on the total number of professional judges per 100,000 inhabitants by collecting data from various public institutions. We draw on those data as a proxy for the historical evolution of ATJ from 1970 through 2014 for a wide range of developed and developing countries. Available upon request.


The Economic Consequences of The Dissolution of French Monasteries
I constructed a new database from both primary and secondary sources on the revenue of Ancien Régime French monasteries. In progress!
